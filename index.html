WHAT'S IT?

The Name says it all. It's the practice of keeping a company or your data safe from malicious attackers with harmfull intentions. Be it hardware or software

As companies get bigger and bigger and generate more and more data, cyber security has become mandatory. Hell yeah job security! Or is it security job...

PREVENTION

Safety is only a feeling. If someone wants to get in, they will get in. It's our main responsibility to implement systems, practices and incident response plans.

These systems hinder and attacker. The longer thet rake, the more time you will have to launch a counter attack!
